About Us

Welcome to Getulawa Dairy Farm! We take pride in producing high-quality dairy products using sustainable and ethical practices.

Getulawa Dairy Farm has been family-owned and operated for generations, and we are committed to providing our customers with the freshest and most delicious dairy products possible.

From our happy and healthy cows grazing on lush pastures to our state-of-the-art production facilities, every step of our process is carefully monitored to ensure the highest standards of quality and freshness.

Our Farm

Welcome to our beautiful Getulawa dairy farm nestled in the countryside. Our farm spans acres of lush green pastures where our cows roam freely, enjoying the fresh air and sunshine.

At our farm, we prioritize the well-being of our animals and the sustainability of our practices. We believe that happy cows produce the best milk, which is why we ensure they are well cared for and have access to nutritious food and clean water.

Our state-of-the-art facilities are designed to maintain the highest standards of hygiene and quality throughout the milk production process. From milking to packaging, every step is carefully monitored to ensure the freshness and purity of our dairy products.
